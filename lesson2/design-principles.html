Design Principles

Lydia Swanson

White Space and Clean Design

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latterday Saints

On the homepage of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latterday Saints, you can see elements of good whitespace used in the header. They chose to keep it simple and plain with very little text. This keeps it fresh and comfortable.

Proximity

Bannana Republic

As seen above, Bannana Republic exemplafies elements of proximity to show what content is related. For example, the "Extra 50% off" is not near the "20 pieces for 2020", so you know that they are not related.

Contrast

Panic Studios

Panic's website is very colorful. They wanted to create a feeling of enjoyment and childlike design. However, they contrast this high color with a simple sans-serif font that keeps things clean and professional. Within the colors you can see the hot pink among the teal background. These contrasting colors draw your eye to the section that they would like you to view.
