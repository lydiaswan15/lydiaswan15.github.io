My Story

Hello! Thank you for visiting my personal website! My name is Lydia and I love creating websites!

In elementary school, my parents would force me to take time everyday to study something new. I always chose to spend time learning some programming, although, if I'm honest, I thought it was a little boring.

When I started my degree, I thought I would still not love it. I reluctantly signed up for a programming class that was required at the time. Instead of being bored throughout the whole class, I found myself looking forward to doing my homework and going to class. I quickly fell in love with the combination of creativity and logic that web development provides.

Since then, I've had the great opportunity to learn so much more about web devleopment and other technologies. I'm looking forward to getting to continue my education during my career!
