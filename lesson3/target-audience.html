Target Audience

My target audience is young women who are between 18-25. They are in college and are members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. They have a busy lifestyle so they need something quick and easy.

Persona 1

Elanor is a student at BYU Idaho. In the morning, she wakes up and realizes that she does not know what the weather will be like today. She opens up our website and sees that it is cloudy and cold. This makes her a little bit sad. However, she looks down and reads the scripture about light from Jesus Christ. This encourages her to continue to have a positive attitude throughout the day.

Persona 2

Kevin has been experiences feelings of depression during the long winter in Provo Utah. He has a date this evening and is trying to decide whether or not he should bring a jacket for her. He comes to our website and sees that it will be a little bit chilly. He also reads the scripture about love and peace through Jesus Christ. This makes him happy and helps him look forward to yet another day. He has a wonderful time.
