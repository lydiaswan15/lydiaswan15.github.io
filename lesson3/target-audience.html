Target Audience

My target audience is young women who are between 18-25. They are in college and are members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. They have a busy lifestyle so they need something quick and easy.

Persona 1

Elanor is a student at BYU Idaho. She is studying Elementary Education. She and her fiance, Kevin like to enjoy time together hunting, fishing, and making home made icecream. In her spare time she goes to the gym, paints, or rides her bike. She loves the gospel and is always looking for ways to come closer to Jesus Christ througout the day.

Persona 2

Sean has been experiences feelings of depression during the long winter in Provo Utah. He has struggled with the church and spent many years of his life away from it. He recently has returned, but feels lonely because he chose not to serve a mission. He wants to feel peace and joy on a daily basis, but does not feel like that will ever happen.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Kevin has a date this evening with a beautiful girl that he has been dying to go out with. A little nervous, he decides to check the weather to see if he should prepare a jacket for her. He looks on the website and finds a scripture about peace and courage through Jesus Christ. He feels motivated and has a wonderful time on his date.

Scenario 2

Shelly was feeling a bit chilly last time she walked to class so she decides to double check the weather this morning. As she does so, she sees that, it is chilly. She also reads the scripture and realizes that she forgot to do her daily scripture study this morning. She does it and feels the spirit!

Scenario 3

George hates cold weather. He checks the weather just to make sure and finds that, alas, it is freezing. He starts to be sad, but reads a scripture that brings him joy again.

Scenario 4

Kate opens her window and sees snow. She needs to know what shoes to wear and so she sees if it will be warm througout the day. She quickly sees that it will warm up and goes about her day.

Scenario 5

Ellen has had a long day at school. She is frusterated, but checks the weather to see what she can do this evening. She sees quickly that it is snowing and realizes that she can go out into the snow!
